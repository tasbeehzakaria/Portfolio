About Me

A Senior student in Public Policy Information Systems at the Faculty of Commerce, Assiut University, currently seeking job opportunities in Cairo. My journey in tech didn’t start with a fixed direction. I explored multiple fields — from Front-End and Back-End development to Cybersecurity — searching for where I truly belong. Through that exploration, I discovered that Data Analysis is where my skills and passion align. I work confidently with Python and its data analysis libraries, and I write advanced SQL queries for data extraction and manipulation. I design ERD and DFD models, clean and preprocess messy datasets, and transform raw data into structured, meaningful insights. Beyond technical accuracy, I focus on presentation. I build dashboards that are not only informative but also visually strong — with thoughtful use of colors, layout, icons, and branding elements. I also have a solid foundation in Statistics and Machine Learning, and I understand how to integrate them into analytical workflows to support smarter decision-making. I transform sales and real estate data into strategic insights that drive smarter pricing, faster sales, and confident decisions. I don’t just analyze data — I translate it into clarity, strategy, and measurable impact.
